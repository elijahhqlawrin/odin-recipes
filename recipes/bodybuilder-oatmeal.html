Bodybuilder's Oatmeal

Recipe Description

An excellent spin on a staple breakfast food that's perfect for if you're looking to gain weight and put on muscle mass.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt, to taste
  • 1-2 scoops whey protein powder

Optional Toppings

Mix and match the following toppings for various flavours.

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin seeds
  • 20 grams dried apricots
  • 1-2 tablespoons natural peanut butter

Steps

  1. Combine oats, while milk, ground cinnamon and salt in a saucepan.
  2. Place over medium heat, stirring occassionally.
  3. Allow the oats to blend uniformly with the milk and cinnamon. Just as the oats begin to bubble, reduce to heat to low and allow the oats to simmer. Stir frequently.
  4. Once it has reached your preferred thickness, remove from heat and pour into serving dish.
  5. Stir in whey protein powder.
  6. Cutomize with toppings of your choice and enjoy.
