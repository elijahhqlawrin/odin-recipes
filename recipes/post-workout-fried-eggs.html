Post Workout Fried Eggs

Recipe Description

Sunny side up fried eggs with a sweet twist: caramalized leeks.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Handful of leeks or onions, chopped
  • salt to taste

Optional Toppings

Mix and match the following toppings and sides for a wide variety of flavours.

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2-3 bacon strips
  • Use lard in place of butter
  • Try my homestyle steak fries

Steps

  1. Add butter and chopped leeks to a large skillet, preferably one with a wide base for best results.
  2. Cook the onions over low heat, stirring occassionally until evenly browned.
  3. Crack eggs into a large bowl.
  4. Pour eggs over top the caramalized leeks.
  5. Keeping the heat low, cover the skillet with a lid and allow eggs to cook slowly.
  6. The eggs are ready to be served once the whites have become solid. The yolks should still be soft and runny.
  7. Add salt or any other seasonings of choice and enjoy.
